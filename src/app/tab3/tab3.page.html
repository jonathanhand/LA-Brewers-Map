L.A. Beer Week What is Beer Week?

L.A. Beer Week was founded in 2009 by a group of dedicated beer enthusiasts and community members. Entering its 11th year in 2019, L.A. Beer Week will be produced by the Los Angeles County Brewers Guild; a 80+ brewery strong non-profit organization dedicated to fostering the culture of #LAbeer. LA Beer Week will feature an L.A. Brewers Beer Week Kickoff festival at Los Angeles Center Studios on Saturday, June 15th which will be the flagship to a week's worth of great events all throughout the City of Angels.
